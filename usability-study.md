# Usability Study

We're opening up the **DeepPhe Visualizer** for a usability study, and we'd love your help. The idea is straightforward: watch how people who work with cancer data actually use the tool — where it clicks, where it gets in the way — and use what we learn to make it better.

The study is still taking shape. Format, scheduling, consent, and the specific tasks are being finalized, so treat this as an early invitation rather than the final word. If you'd like to take part, you can start exploring the live demo today.

## Try the live demo

The [DeepPhe Patient Cohort Explorer]({{ page.app_url }}) is running now with a **synthetic, de-identified 500-patient dataset** — no real patient information is involved. Set aside roughly 30–45 minutes if you'd like to explore it thoroughly.

A few things worth trying:

- **Build a cohort** from the filter cards — combine demographics, cancer type, staging, pathology, biomarkers, and treatment, and watch the patient count update as you go.
- **Review the results** — open the selected patients, sort and search the table, and export a set.
- **Trace a finding back to its source** — open a patient, find a specific result, and follow it back to the clinical note it came from. This last step is the one we're most curious to hear about.

## Share your feedback

For now, the quickest way to weigh in is the [DeepPhe user survey]({{ '/survey/' | relative_url }}). As the study firms up we'll add consent details, a short set of guided tasks, and a way to schedule a session with us. Questions, or interested in taking part? Reach us at [questions@deepphe.org](mailto:questions@deepphe.org).
