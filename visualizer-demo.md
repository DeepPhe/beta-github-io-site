# Visualizer Demo

The **DeepPhe Visualizer Demo** is a browser-based demonstration of the DeepPhe Visualizer. It shows how researchers can build a patient cohort, review the matching patients, and trace extracted clinical information back to the notes that support it.

The demo uses a **synthetic dataset of 500 patients**. It does not contain real patient information.

## Open the demo

[Open the DeepPhe Visualizer Demo]({{ page.app_url }}) in your web browser. There is nothing to download or install. You can explore at your own pace; allow about 30–45 minutes if you would like to try the full workflow.

## What you can do

- **Build a cohort** from the filter cards by combining demographics, cancer type, staging, pathology, biomarkers, and treatment criteria. The matching patient count updates as you work.
- **Review the results** by opening the matching patients, sorting and searching the table, and exporting a patient set.
- **Trace a finding to its source** by opening a patient, locating a specific finding, and following it back to the clinical note it was extracted from.
