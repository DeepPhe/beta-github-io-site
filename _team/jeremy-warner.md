Jeremy L. Warner is a site principal investigator for DeepPhe and a professor of medicine and biostatistics at Brown University. He is also associate director of data science at the Legorreta Cancer Center and an attending physician at the Lifespan Cancer Institute.

His work focuses on clinical and translational cancer informatics, including methods for using structured and unstructured electronic health record data, cancer knowledge bases, clinical data standards, and visualization.
