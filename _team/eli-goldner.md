Eli Goldner is an NLP data scientist at Boston Children's Hospital and a contributor to DeepPhe research.

His DeepPhe-related work includes the development and evaluation of natural language processing methods for extracting radiotherapy events and systemic anticancer therapy timelines from clinical text.
