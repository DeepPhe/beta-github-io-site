John Levander is a DeepPhe contributor responsible for developing DeepPhe Visualizer 2, designing and maintaining the project website, and building the cross-platform DeepPhe installer.

His work also includes the DeepPhe Data API, database pipeline, distribution infrastructure, and supporting software that connects DeepPhe output to usable research tools.
