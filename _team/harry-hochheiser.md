Harry Hochheiser is a site principal investigator for DeepPhe and a professor in the Department of Biomedical Informatics at the University of Pittsburgh.

His research combines biomedical informatics, human-computer interaction, information visualization, and data science to make complex biomedical information more useful. His work on DeepPhe includes information models, cancer registry workflows, and usable tools for exploring clinical data.
