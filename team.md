## Institutions and collaboration

The team members listed here are affiliated with [Boston Children's Hospital](https://www.childrenshospital.org), [Harvard Medical School](https://hms.harvard.edu), the [University of Pittsburgh](https://www.pitt.edu), and [Brown University](https://www.brown.edu). DeepPhe-CR was developed in partnership with the [Kentucky Cancer Registry](https://www.kcr.uky.edu/).

## Funding and support

Work on DeepPhe Translational and DeepPhe Visualizer 2 was supported by the National Cancer Institute's [Information Technology for Cancer Research program](https://itcr.cancer.gov) under grant U24CA248010. Work on DeepPhe-CR was supported by NCI's [Surveillance, Epidemiology, and End Results program](https://seer.cancer.gov) under grant UH3CA243120.
