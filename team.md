<section class="team-support-card" aria-labelledby="institutions-heading">
  <h2 id="institutions-heading">Institutions and collaboration</h2>
  <p>The team members listed here are affiliated with <a href="https://www.childrenshospital.org">Boston Children's Hospital</a>, <a href="https://hms.harvard.edu">Harvard Medical School</a>, the <a href="https://www.pitt.edu">University of Pittsburgh</a>, and <a href="https://www.brown.edu">Brown University</a>. <section class="team-support-card" aria-labelledby="funding-heading">

Work on DeepPhe-CR was supported by NCI's <a href="https://seer.cancer.gov">Surveillance, Epidemiology, and End Results program</a> under grant <a href="https://reporter.nih.gov/project-details/10656293">UH3CA243120</a>.</p>
